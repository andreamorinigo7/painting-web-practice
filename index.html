Painting is a visual art form that involves applying paint, pigment, or other mediums to a solid surface, or "matrix", to express ideas and emotions. Artists use shapes, lines, colors, tones, and textures to create sensations of movement, space, volume, and light on a flat surface. Paintings can represent real or supernatural phenomena, interpret a narrative, or be abstract.
